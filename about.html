Jackson's Bakery

Delighting Taste Buds Since 1948

Welcome to Jackson's Bakery, a cherished institution in Greece, NY, where the aroma of freshly baked goods has filled the air for 75 years.

Established in 1948, Jackson's Bakery has been committed to the time-honored art of crafting exceptional cakes and cookies. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring that each bite is a nostalgic journey through the flavors of yesteryear.

Located in the heart of Greece, NY, our bakery is not just a place to savor delicious treats; it's a community landmark that has been witness to countless celebrations, milestones, and the everyday moments that make life sweet.

At Jackson's Bakery, we take pride in using only the finest ingredients to create mouthwatering cakes and cookies that are baked to perfection. Our bakers, inspired by traditional techniques, infuse every creation with love and a commitment to quality.

Whether you're seeking a custom-designed cake for a special occasion or simply craving the comforting taste of a classic cookie, Jackson's Bakery is the destination for those who appreciate the timeless allure of handcrafted baked goods.

Thank you for being a part of our journey over the past 75 years. We look forward to continuing to serve you with the same passion and dedication that has defined Jackson's Bakery since its inception.
